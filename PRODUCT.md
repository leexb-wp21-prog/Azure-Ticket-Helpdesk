# QuickAid Product Context

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
QuickAid is a university helpdesk portal for students, staff, support agents, and administrators. It supports ticket submission, ticket tracking, knowledge base self-service, service desk triage, assignment workflows, and support team administration.

## Users
Students and staff use the portal to report campus IT, facilities, access, and academic administration issues. Helpdesk administrators use the admin console to monitor service volume, manage queues, assign tickets, and maintain support teams.

## Tone
Professional, calm, institutional, and service-oriented. The interface should feel like a real campus service desk rather than a demo app or AI-generated dashboard.

## Strategic Principles
- Prioritize clear ticket workflow language over marketing language.
- Use restrained color and standard product UI patterns.
- Show operational credibility through statuses, timestamps, SLA cues, filters, and support ownership.
- Remove test-only, demo, and backend implementation copy from user-facing screens.
- Keep the interface practical for repeated use on laptops in an office, library, or service counter.
